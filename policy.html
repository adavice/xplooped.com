PRIVACY AND COOKIE POLICY

Effective as of January 1st 2025

Data Controller

At XPlooped, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our services, including AI coaching and HTML5 games.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data when you interact with our platform, which may include:

  • Information You Provide Directly
    Name, email address, phone number, physical address, payment details.
  • Automatically Collected Data
    IP address, device information, browser type, usage patterns.
  • Third-Party Sources
    Data from trusted partners, such as payment processors, to support transactions and improve services.

2. How We Use Your Data & Recording Sessions

We use the data you provide to deliver, maintain, and improve our services:

  • Personalization: Tailoring AI coaching sessions and game experiences to your skill level and preferences.
  • AI Coaching Analysis: Recording sessions to provide performance feedback and improve AI recommendations.
  • Service Delivery: Ensuring smooth operation of subscriptions, payments, and platform features.
  • Fraud Prevention & Security: Detecting and preventing unauthorized use or abuse of our platform.
  • Communication: Sending updates, notifications, or support messages.
  • Anonymization: Any recordings or gameplay data used for AI improvement are anonymized and cannot be linked to you personally.

Your recordings and data are never shared with third parties for marketing purposes without explicit consent.

3. Why We Collect Your Data

We only collect information necessary to:

  • Deliver and improve AI coaching and game services.
  • Manage your account.
  • Process payments securely.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Communicate important updates.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

4. Compliance with EU AI Act

Xplooped’s AI coaching services comply with the EU AI Act for users in the European Union:

  • Transparency: Users are informed that AI coaching provides advice and strategies; results are not guaranteed.
  • Risk Management: AI models are monitored and tested to ensure safe and responsible use.
  • Accountability: Xplooped maintains policies and technical measures to minimize risks from AI recommendations.
  • User Rights: Users can access information about AI decision-making and report concerns regarding AI coaching.

5. How We Protect Your Data

We implement multiple safeguards to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data, including payment information, is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption (e.g., TLS).
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access personal data when necessary.
  • Security Audits: Systems and processes are regularly tested to identify and fix vulnerabilities.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the information needed to provide services.
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in protected environments with firewalls and other safeguards.

Please also keep your account credentials secure and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized activity.

6. GDPR Compliance

Xplooped complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • Lawful Basis: Personal data is collected and processed based on consent, contractual obligations, or other legitimate interests.
  • Data Security: Encryption, access controls, and secure storage protect your data.
  • Your Rights: You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or portability of your data. Consent can be withdrawn at any time.
  • Complaints: You may lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact support@xplooped.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The revised policy, with an updated “Effective Date”, will be posted on this page.

Continued use of services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Major updates will be communicated at least two weeks in advance.
